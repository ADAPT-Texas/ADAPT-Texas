Join a Design Team

Are you passionate about engineering, design, or advocacy? ADAPT Texas design teams work on hands-on projects that create adaptive technology and support individuals with disabilities. Whether you’re skilled in fabrication or interested in accessibility initiatives, there’s a place for you!

How It Works

  • Choose a Team – Join a project-based engineering team or our advocacy team.
  • Collaborate & Innovate – Work alongside students, faculty, and community partners.
  • Create Impact – Design adaptive solutions or help connect individuals with vital resources.

Current Design Teams

Universal Prosthetic Team

3D-printing and assembling prosthetic limbs.

Customizing designs to meet user needs.

Ride-On Car Adaptation Team

Modifying toy cars for children with mobility impairments.

Implementing custom switches and support systems.

Toys-for-All Team

Adapting toys for children with different abilities.

Enhancing accessibility with 3D-printed components and electronics.

Advocacy & Accessibility Team

Finding and sharing resources for families and students.

Promoting accessibility initiatives on campus and in the community.
