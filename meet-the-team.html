Meet the Team

President

Rocio Hernandez

Major: Electrical and Computer Engineering

Year: 5th Year

Why did you join ADAPT?

I believe engineers should use their skills to make a difference. I hope to inspire others to apply what they’ve learned and experienced in ADAPT to support and empower underserved communities.

Vice President Internal

Ricardo Torres

Major: Biomedical Engineering

Year: 3rd Year

Why did you join ADAPT?

I joined because I knew that the effort we put into the projects goes directly to help a real person. This brings real value to the work our members do, it's not just something fancy you can put in a resume, these efforts are actual advocacy.

Vice President External

Angelina Apodaca

Major: Biomedical Engineering

Year: 4th Year

Why did you join ADAPT?

I joined because I grew up facing life with a disability and parents who would try to adapt many things for me, ranging from pants to sports equipment. I wanted to provide those same efforts, as an engineering student equipped with a variety of skill sets, to those who can also feel empowered and go about their day to day playing with their toys too!

Secretary

Caitlin Kruger

Major: Mechanical Engineering

Year: 2nd Year

Why did you join ADAPT?

I joined ADAPT because it is an organization that really resembled a part of who I am. Growing up, like most kids I always loved toys (and still do), and have so many core memories of building things like Hot Wheel tracks, Lego structures, and more with my Dad and brother. I wanted all kids to be able to experience the joy that comes as a result of the exploration toys allow.

Treasurer

I joined ADAPT TX so I could put the engineering skills I had learned to practical use. ADAPT TX’s work has given context to my course work and given me a chance to be a part of something greater.

Project Lead: Toys-for-All

Bianca Ayala

Major: Mechanical Engineering

Year: 3rd Year

Why did you join ADAPT?

Initially, I joined because I knew some of the leaders, but as I became more familiar with the mission and projects, I quickly realized how meaningful this work truly is. The idea of adapting toys so that children with disabilities can experience the same joy, independence, and creativity as any other child deeply resonated with me. What started as a simple connection to friends grew into a personal commitment to use my skills to create real change in my community.

Project Lead: Advocacy & Accessibility

Abel Worku

Major: Human Development & Family Sciences

Year: 4th Year

Why did you join ADAPT?

I joined ADAPT because I loved the idea of working with families and children. Unlike other engineering organizations that are only concerned with resume building and funding the next big tech innovation, you could see the direct impact ADAPT had on a family's life. Having worked as lead for one of the projects at ADAPT, it is so inspiring to see the next generation of members have that same sense of purpose, a sense of wanting to help and care for others at the core of why they became engineers.
