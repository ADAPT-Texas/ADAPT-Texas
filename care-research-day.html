What Is CARE Research Day?

CARE Research Day is an annual event hosted by our partner research group, CARE (Cellular to Clinically Applied Rehabilitation Research and Engineering). The event serves as a rehabilitation research symposium aimed at fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and advancing the future of patient care. It features a variety of student-led research posters, showcasing rehabilitation innovations from UT and beyond. Each year, the event also features an influential keynote speaker, contributing valuable insights into the field.

ADAPT Texas proudly participates in this exciting event, showcasing the innovative projects our Design Teams have been working on throughout the year. It’s a chance for us to demonstrate the impact of our work and highlight how our solutions improve lives.
