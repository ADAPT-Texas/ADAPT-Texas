How to Donate to ADAPT Texas

Thank you for your interest in supporting ADAPT Texas! Your donation will help us continue our mission of designing and engineering assistive technology to improve accessibility for individuals with disabilities. Every contribution makes a difference!

  1. Visit the Donation Page: Giving to UT
  2. Choose either "One-Time" or "Recurring" and select any donation amount, every dollar helps us continue our impact work!
  3. Under "Designation" click "Cockrell School of Engineering (ENGR)"
  4. Search for “Adapt Texas” and select it.
  5. Complete the Donation Form. Fill in your payment details and any additional information required.
  6. Submit Your Donation. Click Pay Now to finalize your contribution.

Together, we can make a meaningful impact!

Become a Sponsor

Join us in making a difference! By sponsoring ADAPT Texas, you support projects and events that create real-world solutions for individuals with disabilities. Your contributions help fund adaptive technology, community outreach, and student engagement activities.
