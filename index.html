ADAPT TEXAS

Welcome to
ADAPT Texas!

Engineering Accessibility,
Empowering Lives

ADAPT Texas is a student-led organization at the University of Texas at Austin dedicated to adapting tools and technology for individuals with disabilities.

Want to get involved? Join us in making a difference!

Our Projects

Ride-on Car

Adapting ride-on toy cars to be more comfortable and accessible for children with mobility challenges making play inclusive and joyful for everyone.

View Project Details

Toys-for-All

Year-round adaptive toy engineering, designing and delivering customized modifications so children with disabilities can play independently and experience joy.

View Project Details

Universal Prosthetic

Combines 3D-printed parts and micro-motors to deliver affordable, fully actuable prosthetics adaptable to varied limb differences, empowering users with greater mobility and independence.

View Project Details

Advocacy & Accessibility

Dedicated to forging partnerships between ADAPT and community organizations to dismantle structural barriers and enhance access to care and resources for UT students and partnered families.

View Project Details
