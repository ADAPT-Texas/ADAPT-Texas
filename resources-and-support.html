Resources & Support

ADAPT Texas is committed to connecting families and students with the resources they need to navigate disability-related challenges. Whether you're looking for assistive technology, community support, or educational resources, this page serves as a hub for essential services in the Austin area.

Assistive Technology & Adaptive Equipment

UT Media Captioning Service

Website Link

Accessible and affordable captioning options for all forms of media. Can be used for content outside of UT’s domain as well.

TAMALES Device Loans

Website Link

Service that provides anyone in need with assistive technology to try out on a short term basis. If you need help finding the right tech for all your accessibility needs, this is the best place to start.

Medical & Therapy Services

UT Physical Therapy

Website Link

UT offers Physical therapy services to its students in partnership with the University Health Services department here on campus. From injury intervention to mobility strengthening, UT Physical Therapists are dedicated to helping you train into a healthier version of yourself.

Educational Support & Advocacy

Report a Barrier

Website Link

UT’s official form for reporting any issues with accessibility tools here on campus. From doors to broken video streams, if you run into an issue, please fill out this form

DCC Newsletter

Website Link

Sign up for the disability cultural centers monthly newsletter. It's the best way to stay informed on programs and resources that are offered by UT.

Sign Up as a Volunteer Notetaker

Website Link

Are you good at taking notes? Sign up to be a Volunteer Notetaker! Volunteer notetakers help students in your class with disability accommodations access notes for lectures.

Community & Support Groups

UT SHARE Communities

Website Link

Therapy groups run by CMHC Care professionals and student peers. All students are welcome to join and listen in or be heard for a multitude of areas of concern or interest.

Disability Advocacy Student Coalition

Website Link

The Disability Advocacy Student Coalition (DASC) is a student organization that strives to advocate for accessibility and equity at UT Austin and beyond, and to offer spaces for disability connection and conversation. People with and without disabilities are welcome!

Employment & Independent Living

IAA Advocacy Team

Website Link

Legal team that works for students with disabilities to make sure that their needs are being met and respected by their employers. From hiring to work environment concerns, feel free to reach out to the IAA legal team for your needs.
